Separation of Duties Rules
Enforced
Initiator cannot approve — The person who submits an upgrade, suspension, or offboarding request cannot be an approver on the same request.
Enforced
Multi-party approval — Plan upgrades require approval from Sales (contract), Finance (billing), and Platform Lead (capacity). All three must approve.
Enforced
Break-glass audit — Super Admin actions require MFA re-verification, written justification, and are auto-escalated to Platform Lead via PagerDuty.
Enforced
Quota edits for Business tier blocked — Business plan quotas are fixed. Changing limits requires a plan upgrade through the approval chain.
